Safety Guidelines

How to stay safe while trading on Barter Express

Before Trading

Verified User Accounts

All Barter Express users undergo identity verification before they can trade. We verify government-issued IDs and cross-check information to prevent fraud and ensure accountability.

Item Verification & Approval

Every item listed on Barter Express is manually reviewed and approved by our team. We verify item authenticity, condition, and accuracy of descriptions. Only approved items can be traded.

Fair Valuations

Our professional appraisers verify the fair market value of every item. This ensures that credit awards are fair and prevent disputes over item value.

Secure Credit System

Your Barter Credits are securely stored in your account. Credits can only be used through our platform for approved items. All transactions are recorded and traceable.

Secure Delivery Management

Platform Handles All Arrangements

Barter Express coordinates all pickups and deliveries on your behalf. You never need to arrange logistics directly or contact other users about delivery.

Professional Courier Partners

We use vetted, insured courier services to handle all item transfers. Every delivery is tracked in real-time so you know exactly where your item is.

Item Insurance

All items in transit are automatically insured through our courier partners. If an item is lost or damaged during delivery, you receive compensation.

Delivery Confirmation

You'll receive notifications at each stage: dispatch, in-transit, and delivery. Confirm receipt in the app to complete your transaction. Disputes can be filed if items don't match descriptions.

Verified Credits

Credits are only awarded after your item is verified and approved. Credits cannot be refunded to external accounts - they can only be used to purchase items on Barter Express.

Transaction Escrow

When you acquire an item, credits are held securely until you confirm receipt. If there's a dispute, our support team can mediate and adjust credits if needed.

No Wire Transfers

Barter Express never requires wire transfers, prepayments, or off-platform transactions. All payments are handled through our secure credit system.

Account Security

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Enable two-factor authentication on your account
  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Log out from shared computers
  • Update your password regularly
  • Keep your email address secure
  • Monitor your account activity regularly
  • Report suspicious login attempts immediately
  • Verify email addresses that contact you from the platform

Report Suspicious Activity

Report Fraudulent Listings

If you encounter suspicious behavior, false listings, or concerning users, use the report feature. Provide details about the concerning activity and our moderation team will investigate.

Contact Support Immediately

Report scams, harassment, fraudulent items, or safety concerns to our support team right away. We take these matters seriously and investigate promptly.

Transaction Issues

Dispute any transaction where the item doesn't match its description or doesn't arrive. Our support team will review delivery records and may compensate you or send a replacement.

Community Standards

Help keep Barter Express safe by reporting abusive users, suspicious behavior, and policy violations. Our moderation team acts swiftly on credible reports.
