Safety Guidelines

How to stay safe while trading on Barter Express

Before Trading

Verify User Profiles

Check the trader's profile, ratings, and reviews before agreeing to a trade. Look for verified badges and established trading history.

Inspect Item Photos Carefully

Request clear, detailed photos from multiple angles. Ask questions about the item's condition, functionality, and any defects.

Use the Platform Messaging

Always communicate through Barter Express messaging. Never share personal contact information or move conversations off-platform until after the trade is complete.

Agree on Trade Details

Be crystal clear about what's being traded, the condition of items, and delivery method. Document all agreements in writing through the platform.

Safe In-Person Meetings

  • Meet in a public, well-lit location (mall, coffee shop, police station)
  • Bring a friend or family member with you
  • Go during daylight hours
  • Tell someone where you're going and when you'll return
  • Inspect items thoroughly before agreeing to trade
  • Test electronics and functionality before the exchange
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, walk away
  • Use mobile payment apps rather than cash when possible
  • Take photos of the items and the other person (with permission)

Red Flags to Watch For:

✗ Pressure to trade quickly
✗ Requests to pay in advance or wire money
✗ Unwillingness to meet in public
✗ Vague or inconsistent item descriptions
✗ New accounts with no trading history
✗ Requests to keep the transaction secret

Shipping Safely

Use Tracked Shipping

Always use shipping services with tracking numbers. This provides proof of delivery and protects both parties.

Pack Items Securely

Use appropriate packaging materials. Take photos of items before packing and include them in your shipment record.

Insure Valuable Items

For high-value trades, purchase shipping insurance. This provides recourse if items are lost or damaged in transit.

Document Everything

Take photos and videos of items before shipping. Keep all receipts, tracking information, and delivery confirmation.

Communicate Throughout

Send tracking information to the other trader. Update them on shipping status and expected delivery date.

Payment Security

Never Send Money First

Don't wire money or send payment before receiving the item. Use Barter Express's secure payment system.

Avoid Overpayment

Be suspicious of requests to pay more than agreed and refund the difference. This is a common scam.

Use Secure Payment Methods

Prefer payment methods with buyer protection. Avoid wire transfers, gift cards, or cryptocurrency that can't be reversed.

Verify Payment Information

Double-check all payment details before sending money. Watch out for similar-looking account numbers or email addresses.

Account Security

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Enable two-factor authentication on your account
  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Log out from shared computers
  • Update your password regularly
  • Keep your email address secure
  • Monitor your account activity regularly
  • Report suspicious login attempts immediately
  • Verify email addresses that contact you from the platform

Report Suspicious Activity

Use Our Report Feature

If you encounter suspicious behavior, use the report feature on the user's profile. Provide details about the concerning activity.

Contact Support Immediately

Report scams, harassment, or safety concerns to our support team right away. We take these matters seriously and investigate promptly.

Document Evidence

Take screenshots of conversations, listings, and any evidence of fraud or harmful behavior. Save all communications related to the incident.

File a Dispute

If a trade doesn't go as agreed, use our dispute resolution process. We'll help mediate and reach a fair resolution.
